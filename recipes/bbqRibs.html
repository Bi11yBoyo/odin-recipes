BBQ Ribs


Description

This recipe is great for family gatherings or impressing the bbq lovers in your life!

This is pretty simple and has a roughly 1 hour prep time.

Ingredients for the sauce

  • 1/2 teaspoon of garlic salt
  • 1 cup of ketchup
  • 1/2 cup of honey(scant)
  • 2 tablespoons of worcestershire sauce
  • 1 and 1/2 teaspoons of liquid smoke
  • 1/2 teaspoon of pepper
  • 1/2 cup of brown sugar
  • 1/4 cup of spicy brown mustard

Additional ingredients

  • 1 rack of ribs
  • 1 crock pot with water

Steps

  1. First, put the ribs in the crockpot and begin boiling for one hour.
  2. Keep cooking if it is not yet tender.
  3. Next, Grill, and baste with sauce occasionally.
  4. Lastly, allow to cool and serve with leftover sauce on the side.
