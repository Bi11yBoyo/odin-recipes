Potato Salad


Description

This recipe is great for hot summer days and BBQ cookouts!

This recipe doesn't take too long to make but it takes a while to cool down so make ahead of time.

Ingredients

  • 6 whole potatoes
  • 2 teaspoons of lemon pepper
  • 1 teaspoon of salt(add to taste)
  • 2 tablespoons of dill relish
  • 1/2 cup of mayo
  • 2 tablespoons of italian dressing
  • 2 tablespoons of yellow mustard

Steps

  1. First, Boil the potatoes until tender.
  2. Then, cut up the potatoes into 1/2 cubes roughly
  3. Next, mix in all of the other ingredients.
  4. Lastly, allow to cool in a refridgerator, then serve.
