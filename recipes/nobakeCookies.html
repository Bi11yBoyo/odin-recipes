No Bake Cookies


Description

This recipe is very popular with the family. It is not only simple to make but also delicious!

This recipe takes a while for the cookies to solidify so make sure to account for cooling time in order to properly consume them.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups of sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoons of salt
  • 1/2 cup of milk
  • 1/4 cup of cocoa
  • 1 stick of margarine
  • 1/2 cup of peanut butter
  • 3 cups of oatmeal
  • 1 teaspoon of vanilla

Instructions

  1. First, Boil the sugar, salt, milk, cocoa, and margarine together for 1 minute.
  2. Then, add peanut butter, oatmeal, and vanilla.
  3. Next, put form into teaspoon sized balls onto wax paper.
  4. Lastly, allow to cool on a open table or a refridgerator until solid.
